Product details

This is the Platinum selling #1 UK follow-up to her platinum debut 'Frank,' which established her as one of the most exciting and challenging artists in pop music, on which Amy confirms, beyond any reasonable or unreasonable doubt, what a truly remarkable talent she is. While the girl-groups of the 60's to which she had become enthralled contained their vocals, Amy can break loose with Aretha-style vocal styling's on Just Friends or by turning the whole idea of drying out into a gospel spiritual on the stunning opener Rehab. Amy's refined song-writing approach and fearlessness as a lyric writer has been grafted into some of the most astonishing material of her short career so far.
